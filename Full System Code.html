        Frequently Asked Questions

        How do I reset my Financify password?

        To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

        How can I track my upcoming bills?

        Open the Expenses tab and use the Bills & Reminders section to add recurring or one-time bills. You'll see them on the calendar and in your dashboard summary.

        Is my Financify financial data secure?

        Yes, we prioritize the security of your financial data. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

        How do I set up a new Financify financial goal?

        Navigate to the "Goals" section of your dashboard. Click on "Add New Goal", and you'll be guided through the process of defining your goal, target amount, and timeline.
